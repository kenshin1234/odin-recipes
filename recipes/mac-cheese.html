Mac & Cheese

Description:

This recipe will tell you how to make Mac & Cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni elbows or shells
  • cheddar cheese
  • butter
  • Salt
  • Milk

Steps for Making Mac & Cheese

  1. 1 cup of water
  2. Poor in pot
  3. add salt
  4. Put Macaroni or elbows in pot
  5. Set stove at high heat
  6. let pot come to a boil
  7. let it boil for 5 mins
  8. Lower temp
  9. add Milk
  10. add butter
  11. add cheese
  12. Mix it
  13. Server it

Turkey Sandwich Recipe

Description:

This recipe will tell you how to make a simple Turkey Sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • Sliced White Bread
  • Sliced Turkey
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonaise
  • Honey Mustard
  • Pickle

Steps for Making the Turkey Sandwich

  1. Put Mayo on Slice Bread
  2. Put Turkey on Slice Bread
  3. Put lettuce on
  4. Put Tomato on
  5. put Pickle on
  6. Put honey mustard on other piece of bread
  7. Put bread on top

Pizza Recipe

Description:

This recipe will tell you how to make a simple delilious cheese pizza.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Fresh Mozeralla Cheese
  • Salt
  • leafs
  • Oregano

Steps for Making the Pizza

  1. Kneed the Dough
  2. Use a roller to make it flat
  3. Apply Tomato sauce
  4. Put Fresh Mozeralla on it
  5. Sprinkle the Sea Salt
  6. Sprinkle some Oregano
  7. Apply olive oil
